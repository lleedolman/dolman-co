1. About Dolman & Co.

Dolman & Co. (ABN 46 775 837 936) is a registered trade mark attorney practice based in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia. We are registered with IP Australia to act as trade mark attorneys. We are not solicitors and do not provide general legal advice. Our services are limited to the scope of trade mark law and related intellectual property matters as described in these terms.

2. Scope of Services

Dolman & Co. provides the following services:

  • Trade mark searches and clearance reports
  • Trade mark filing and prosecution before IP Australia
  • Trade mark portfolio audits and brand protection strategy
  • Drafting and reviewing trade mark licence and assignment agreements
  • Enforcement advice and cease and desist correspondence
  • Representation in IP Australia opposition and hearing proceedings
  • Ongoing brand protection retainer services

The specific scope of work for your matter will be set out in your engagement letter or quote. We do not provide services outside the agreed scope without your written instruction and agreement on fees.

We do not provide advocacy services in the Federal Court of Australia or any other court. Where litigation is required, we will advise you to engage a solicitor and can assist with referrals.
